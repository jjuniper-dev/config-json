THE CORE QUESTION
─────────────────
Should PATH (HC-led governance control plane) and HAIL (PHAC-led AI runtime)
CONVERGE into a single platform or remain CO-EQUAL with defined integration points?

Answer determines ARB decision on enterprise AI platform strategy and shadow AI risk
mitigation.


DELIVERABLES (In Priority Order)
─────────────────────────────────

P1 — Convergence Framing Document (001-convergence-framing.md)
   → 2000–2500 words
   → Three explicit scenarios (A: Converge, B: Co-Equal, C: Defer PATH)
   → EA-recommended position with tradeoff analysis
   → Open dependencies listed
   → OUTPUT: Direct use in ARB briefing

P2 — WAF Assessment (002-waf-assessment.md)
   → 1500–2000 words
   → Operational Excellence and Reliability pillar analysis
   → Table showing gaps, current state, scenario feasibility
   → Evidence backing P1 framing
   → OUTPUT: ARB evidence, stakeholder validation

P3 — Risk Register (003-risk-register.md)
   → 1000–1500 words
   → Five key convergence risks with current mitigation, residual risk, scenario impact
   → Escalation triggers for each risk
   → OUTPUT: Monitoring dashboard, escalation narrative

P4 — ADR-002: Protected B Hosting (004-adr-002.md)
   → 800–1200 words
   → Resolves PATH hosting decision (Azure.us vs. Commercial vs. Hybrid)
   → Linked from P1 document
   → OUTPUT: ARB-ready decision record

P5 — Shadow AI Narrative (005-shadow-ai-narrative.md)
   → 800–1200 words
   → Business risk frame: why workloads route to ungoverned platforms
   → Use case recognition (PMRA, Surveillance, etc.)
   → Why convergence decision matters to institution
   → OUTPUT: DM/ADM briefing material


ANALYTICAL FRAMEWORK
────────────────────
Baseline: Azure Well-Architected Framework (WAF)

Primary Assessment Pillars:
  • Operational Excellence — Integration feasibility, FinOps unification
  • Reliability — RTO/RPO targets, shared dependency risk

Secondary Dimensions:
  • Governance Coherence — Can policies be enforced consistently?
  • Data Residency & Sovereignty — Unclassified vs. Protected B constraints
  • FinOps Clarity — Cost attribution per scenario
  • Shadow AI Risk — Which scenario delivers governance fastest?
